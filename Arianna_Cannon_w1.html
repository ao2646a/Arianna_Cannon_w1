Cannon Collection

Introducing Ferrari Cannon Designs. We have worked hard to combine modern feminism with our sewing classics. With the help of past generations, an old mean green sewing machine, and a unique twist on femininity, our class collection has come to be.

With Ferrari Cannon Designs you have a choice to be bold. The magic is in the detail. Drawing from artists such as Araki Koman, our fabrics and designs emphazise the beauty of using a multitude of different materials. Come explore our collection.
